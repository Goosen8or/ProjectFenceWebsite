WHY CHOOSE CLEAR VIEW

The best perimeter protection

Clear View Fencing is fast becoming the leading choice in the fencing industry, overtaking its competitors in palisade fencing, it has become a popular fencing option because of its solid design and high visibility

 

Anti-Climb and Anti-Cut

The aperture of Clear View panels, 76x12mm, makes it virtually impossible to climb. With the space being a 12mm gap, it becomes pratically impossible to cut or use bolt cutters.

 

Match your colours

Clear View Fencing with Project Fence hosts a wide range of options to choose from including height, colour and finish, to ensure your fencing not only provides the desired security benefits but also compliments your home design.

 

Aesthetically Pleasing

Project Fence's Clear View Fencing offers high visibilty or see through appearance when one looks at the mesh panel, creating a sense of freedom.

ATTACHMENTS

ANTI-TAMPER SCREW

Anti-tamper screws are available as self-drillers (in galvanized or stainless steel) as part of a rivet solution and used with Square Tube posts during installing. They can only be applied or removed with a uniquely designed key.

SHEAR OFF NUTS AND BOLTS

This system is used for application to the IPE posts and prevents subsequent removal by its shear off ability. Available in galvanised and stainless steel.

FIXATOR

Our uniquely designed fixating solution ensures maximum wire contact is achieved at all times, providing customers with a strong, reliable and secure barrier solution that protects their property.
